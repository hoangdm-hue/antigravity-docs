# Conversation View - Panes


---

> Panes Panes You can open files, artifacts, knowledge items, and other content directly within the manager in panes that persist per-conversation. In order to open up a pane, simply open up the quick picker (by hitting CMD+P on Mac or CTRL+P on Windows/Linux) and select a resource. You can also hit the "+" from within a conversation's header. 1/10
